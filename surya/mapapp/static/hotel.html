VILLUPURAM DINDUGAL THALAPPAKATTI BRIYANI...

            
If you're in Villupuram, the Dindigul Thalappakatti Biryani spot is a must-try. 
                Famous for its seeraga samba rice and rich, flavorful meat, it serves up that classic Dindigul taste 
                that biryani lovers swear by. Simple, authentic, and seriously tasty.
